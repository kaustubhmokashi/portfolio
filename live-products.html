B2C SaaS Platform

Paperpal Edit

Paperpal is an AI academic writing platform that helps researchers and students read smarter, write better, edit faster, and submit with more confidence. It brings language support, AI writing, research and citation, plagiarism checks, and submission-readiness workflows into one experience.

I’m the lead designer for this platform and manage both the feature direction and the overall experience, working with a team across the product. As of April 5, 2026, Paperpal publicly states that it is used by 4M+ academics across 125+ countries, has worked on 10B+ words of academic text, and is trusted by 1,500+ journals worldwide.

B2C EdTech SaaS

Simplilearn LMS

I led the learner experience for Simplilearn’s Learning Management System, shaping the information architecture, dashboard, learning journeys, Help & Support, and community experience across a large-scale, login-gated learning platform.

Through learner research and targeted UX initiatives driven by a POD team, we increased course completion from 35% to 50%, improved Help & Support CSAT from 3.2 to 4.1, and grew community monthly active users from around 80 to 1000. I also led the Skillup domain, growing revenue without marketing involvement and making it the only profitable department.
