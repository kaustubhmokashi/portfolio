Simplilearn LMS

I led the learner experience for Simplilearn’s Learning Management System, shaping the information architecture, dashboard, learning journeys, Help & Support, and community experience across a large-scale, login-gated learning platform.

Through learner research and targeted UX initiatives driven by a POD team, we increased course completion from 35% to 50%, improved Help & Support CSAT from 3.2 to 4.1, and grew community monthly active users from around 80 to 1000. I also led the Skillup domain, growing revenue without marketing involvement and making it the only profitable department.

Luminous India

Luminous India is a direct ecommerce platform for power backup and residential solar products. The work focused on helping more users discover the right products and complete purchases directly on the brand’s own platform.

At the time, a large share of sales was flowing through Flipkart and Amazon. I helped restructure the ecommerce experience to improve discoverability and sales without relying on extra discounting, working alongside one junior designer.

Viva Konnect

Viva Konnect is an IoT-connected patient app within Vivance’s kidney-care ecosystem, designed to support therapy tracking, medication adherence, vitals monitoring, dietary management, teleconsultation, and remote connectivity with care providers.

I was the sole designer on this project for a Singapore-based company. Alongside the patient app, the wider ecosystem included a clinic portal and AI-based risk prediction models. The app was tied to the physical product experience and shared on invitation after purchase rather than through the public app stores.
